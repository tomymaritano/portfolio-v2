Design Notes

  • Hero: Avatar + name left-aligned, tagline big, two CTAs (primary/secondary)
  • Projects: 2-col grid cards with status badge, tech tags in monospace
  • Blog: Simple list rows — title + date. No cards, no images. Clean.
  • Experience: Date in monospace left column, role + company right
  • CTA: Centered, minimal. Single button.
  • Dividers: Gradient fade lines between sections
  • No gradients, no glow, no noise texture. Pure structure + type.
